STATE ACTOR HARASSMENT (CSIS, 2025; CNN, 2026)

Commercial Vessel Seizures

State actors frequently harass or seize commercial vessels under legal pretexts. In reality, these are retaliatory actions linked to sanctions enforcement (CSIS, 2025). Recent CNN (2026) reports confirm multiple IRGC fast-attack craft interceptions this week, forcing international navies to escalate escort missions.

Economic Consequence

Spikes maritime insurance premiums by up to 300% for passing vessels. These multi-million dollar overhead costs are immediately passed down to global consumers via inflation (CSIS, 2025; Al Jazeera, 2026).

Geopolitical Implication

Forces international naval coalitions into direct proximity with Iranian fast-attack craft, sharply escalating the risk of accidental kinetic engagement and retaliatory sanctions.
